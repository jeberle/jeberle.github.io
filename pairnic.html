PairNIC

After dealing w/ el cheapo domain names + hosting services, I decided to step up a bit and go w/ PairNIC. PairNIC is the domain name registrar for Pair, a FreeBSD hosting company.

PairNIC is a major improvement from APlus.net & 1and1. The latter two enjoy spamming you both by email or even more annoyingly while using their site. They’re breaking a rule somewhere that it’s incredibly tacky to spam your own customers.

Anyway, so far PairNIC has been great. Great team, great site, no SPAM. That’s really all I want.
